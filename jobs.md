# Job Title: Curriculum Scientist
**Location:** IIT Ropar, Punjab, India  
**Salary:** INR 1.2 Lakhs per Monthh  
**Working With:** Prof. Sudarshan (Head - AI Minor Initiative, IIT Ropar)  
**Project:** Minor in AI Program  

## Job Description
We are seeking a dedicated and innovative Curriculum Scientist to join our team for the Minor in AI Program at IIT Ropar. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in artificial intelligence and a passion for education. You will collaborate closely with Prof. Sudarshan to develop and enhance curriculum content, improve pedagogical approaches, and deliver online sessions to thousands of students.

### Responsibilities:
- **Curriculum Creation:** Develop comprehensive curriculum materials for the Minor in AI Program, including lectures, assignments, and assessments.
- **Pedagogy Improvement:** Implement innovative teaching methods to enhance student engagement and learning outcomes.
- **Online Sessions:** Conduct live online sessions effectively, communicating complex AI concepts to a large audience.
- **Collaboration:** Work closely with faculty members to ensure the curriculum aligns with program objectives and industry standards.
- **Continuous Learning:** Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI to incorporate new knowledge into the curriculum.

### Qualifications:
- **Education:** MTech or PhD in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field.
- **Knowledge:** Strong understanding of AI principles, machine learning algorithms, and data science.
- **Skills:** Excellent communication and presentation skills; proficiency in creating educational content.
- **Experience:** Prior experience in teaching or curriculum development is a plus.
- **Adaptability:** Ability to engage large groups of students in an online learning environment.

### Hiring Process:
1. **Application Screening:** Review of submitted resumes and cover letters to assess eligibility and qualifications.
2. **Technical Interview:** Evaluation of AI knowledge, teaching philosophy, and curriculum development skills.
3. **Final Interview:** Interaction with the hiring committee, including Prof. Sudarshan, to discuss fit and expectations.
