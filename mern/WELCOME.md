# Welcome to the Interview Chat

Hello! 👋

Welcome to our internship program chat assistant. I'm here to help you with any questions you might have about the internship opportunity.

---

## Important Information Before We Begin

**Please read the following carefully:**

1. **Phone Number Required:** Your phone number is compulsory for identification and communication purposes. We will ask for it during this chat. Please have it ready.

2. **Session Duration:** This chat session has a maximum time limit of **30 minutes**. Please ensure you have enough time to complete your queries.

3. **Response Time:** If you don't respond for **5 minutes**, the session will automatically end.

4. **Scope:** I can only answer questions related to the internship opportunity. Questions outside this scope cannot be addressed.

5. **Resume Required:** You MUST upload your **resume/CV** during this chat. It must be in **PDF format only**. If you don't have a resume ready, we cannot proceed with this chat.

---

## What I Can Help With

- Questions about the internship role and responsibilities
- Application and eligibility details
- Interview process and timeline
- Any other internship-related queries

---

## How It Works

- Feel free to ask me anything about the internship
- If I don't know the answer, I'll ask for your email so we can follow up with an answer later
- New questions will be marked with #newquestion and added to our FAQ
- I may ask you to confirm your phone number and email by typing them again
