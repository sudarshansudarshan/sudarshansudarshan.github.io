## Application Process

### How do I apply for the internship?
You can apply by visiting our careers page and submitting your application along with your resume and any required documents. Our team will review your application and get back to you.

### What documents are required for application?
Typically, you need to submit your resume, cover letter (if applicable), and any relevant certificates or portfolios. Check the specific job posting for detailed requirements.

### Is there a deadline to apply?
Yes, each internship posting has a specific deadline. We recommend applying before the deadline to ensure your application is considered.

---

## Eligibility

### What are the eligibility criteria?
The eligibility criteria varies by position. Generally, we look for students in their penultimate or final year of studies. Specific requirements like CGPA, skills, and domain knowledge are mentioned in each job posting.

### What skills are required?
Required skills differ based on the role. Technical internships may require programming knowledge, problem-solving skills, and relevant project experience. Soft skills like communication and teamwork are valued across all roles.

### Can I apply if I'm in my first year of studies?
Most of our internships target students in their second or third year (or penultimate year). However, some entry-level positions may be open to first-year students. Check the specific posting for eligibility.

---

## Internship Details

### What is the duration of the internship?
The internship duration typically ranges from 3 to 6 months, depending on the role and project requirements.

### Is this a paid internship?
Yes, most of our internships are paid. The stipend amount varies based on the role, location, and candidate's experience level.

### Will there be a chance of conversion to a full-time role?
Yes, top-performing interns often get converted to full-time employees. This depends on both your performance during the internship and available positions.

---

## Interview Process

### What is the interview process?
Our interview process usually consists of:
1. Initial screening (resume review)
2. Technical interview(s)
3. HR/Managerial round

The exact process may vary by role.

### How long does the selection process take?
The entire process typically takes 2-4 weeks from the application date to the final offer.

### Will there be a coding round?
For technical roles, yes. The coding round evaluates your problem-solving skills and technical knowledge.

---

## Work & Culture

### What technologies will I work with?
The technologies vary by team. We work with modern tech stacks including React, Node.js, Python, Java, cloud platforms, and more.

### Will I get mentorship during the internship?
Yes, every intern is assigned a mentor who will guide you through your projects and help you learn.
